About Me

When I'm not writing SQL scripts or analysing large data sets, you can find me doing something with football. I play semi-professionally in the NSW Premier League. I also enjoy watching football and playing the latest FIFA on the Playstation.



I'm also a firm believer in lifelong learning and have always got a course, project or book on the side each week. I almost only read non-fiction books to be honest. On the topic of courses, I can vouch for the courses on DataCamp & Udemy since they both give a good balance in theory but also practical application with projects and coding. Look out for a recommended book/course section in the near future.

One of my goals is to be able to teach data science or analytics to others, whether that be an online course or in a University setting. I think when people start out in this field there is so many questions that go unanswered till later on and it can be quite intimidating having that feeling so I feel I could help new starters on that journey. Further, having researched meta-learning too, I think the best way to learn something is to teach it.
